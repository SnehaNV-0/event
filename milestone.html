Marquee & Stretch Tents

Planning a garden party at home? We’ve organized numerous luxurious parties for our clients using their back gardens. Using a marquee as a party venue allows for a wide range of options, from extravagant and elaborate to sleek and sophisticated.

Our marquees are beautifully styled with draping and lighting and come equipped with heating, flooring, and more.

We offer a wide range of event structures that provide flexibility and ample space for guests. We specialize in creating luxurious marquees and multi-zoned structures:

  • DJ/Band Areas for dancing
  • Children Play Areas
  • House Extensions
